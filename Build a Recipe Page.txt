      <p class="intro">A quick, flavorful vegetarian wrap filled with spiced paneer, crisp veggies, and a tangy yogurt chutney — perfect for lunch or a light dinner.</p>

  <section>
    <h2>Ingredients</h2>
    <ul>
      <li>200g paneer, cubed</li>
      <li>2 whole-wheat tortillas or wraps</li>
      <li>1 small onion, thinly sliced</li>
      <li>1 cup mixed bell peppers, sliced</li>
      <li>2 tbsp yogurt + 1 tsp chutney (for dressing)</li>
      <li>Spice mix: 1 tsp garam masala, 1/2 tsp chili powder, salt to taste</li>
    </ul>
  </section>

  <section>
    <h2>Instructions</h2>
    <ol>
      <li>Marinate paneer cubes with yogurt, garam masala, chili powder, and salt for 10 minutes.</li>
      <li>Sauté sliced onions and bell peppers in a pan until softened; set aside.</li>
      <li>Pan-fry the marinated paneer on medium heat until golden on all sides.</li>
      <li>Warm the tortillas, spread a thin layer of chutney-yogurt, add paneer and veggies, then roll tightly.</li>
      <li>Slice the wrap in half and serve immediately with extra chutney on the side.</li>
    </ol>
  </section>
